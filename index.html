Ethereum Charities


Information



EtherGive bridges the gap between traditional charities, with Ethereum. Using EtherGive, people can now anonymously donate to organizations, such as The Red Cross, and The Water Project.

Why us? We directly work for several charities. We generate a new wallet for each donor, to provide anonymity. The collected money is sent to the charities every 2 weeks, so you won't have to worry about it being too late.

We keep all of the funds, in ethereum, until the charity transfers the ether, into local fiat. If Ethereum significantly drops in value, we will open a poll, to decide to hold the ether, or convert it to fiat in 2 weeks.
